---
title: "Async Systems Over Clever Code"
description: "Why production systems win when work is durable, observable, and retryable."
date: "2026-07-01"
category: "Node.js"
published: true
---

The systems that survive production rarely depend on clever request/response paths.

They depend on:

- clear boundaries
- durable work
- retries with idempotency
- observability that matches failure modes

Queues are not an optimization for later. For payment and AI workloads, they are often the architecture.
